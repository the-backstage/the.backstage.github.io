Waiting For Godot (Erik Whien)

The National Theatre

Theatre
Tue 23 Apr 2024

Two men, Vladimir and Estragon, sit by a tree on a country road. They wait for a certain Godot and kill their time with big questions and small pursuits. A certain Pozzo comes along, dragging another man, Lucky, on a rope. When they leave after a strange conversation, there is nothing to do but wait on. Meanwhile, time passes. Night is coming, a new day is dawning. In ignorance, Vladimir and Estragon continue to wait, "because if he comes, then we are saved".
