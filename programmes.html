Waiting For Godot (Erik Whien)

The National Theatre

Theatre
Tue 23 Apr 2024

Two men, Vladimir and Estragon, sit by a tree on a country road. They wait for a certain Godot and kill their time with big questions and small pursuits. A certain Pozzo comes along, dragging another man, Lucky, on a rope. When they leave after a strange conversation, there is nothing to do but wait on. Meanwhile, time passes. Night is coming, a new day is dawning. In ignorance, Vladimir and Estragon continue to wait, "because if he comes, then we are saved".

Geubels

Cabaret

Marlene

Sven Ratzke

Musical
Sat 30 Mar 2024

It is November 3, 1960. Marlene is back in Berlin. She's in the locker room. Outside, the crowd chants "Go home Marlene!" and "Traitor!" Inside, the diva is preparing for her first show since the war. What follows is a journey through the life of one of the greatest icons of the 20th century. A glimpse into the madness, the showbiz, her memories full of nostalgia and longing. The highlight was the concert as she gave it then, in 1960 in Berlin.
