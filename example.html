Our brand stands for authenticity and comfort, and we can only reach that outcome with honesty.

The Backstage

We chose “The Backstage” for our Brand’s name, because in theatres even if the show is on the stage there is a lot of hard work going on in the backstage that the audience does not get to experience.

This way, we are able to expand the theatre experience, without secrets, just authenticity.
