Welcome back, Evelyn!


Evelyn

Age: 55

Region: Noord-Branbant

Fun Fact: "I once performed in a local community theater production of 'A Midsummer Night's Dream' and played the role of Titania, the Queen of the Fairies."

Best Play: "The Phantom of the Opera" by Andrew Lloyd Webber

Bio

Hello! I'm a devoted mother and wife who finds joy in nurturing my family and cherishing every moment with them. At 55, I've embraced the beauty of life's journey, finding solace and inspiration not only in literature but also in the captivating world of theatre. With a background in English literature and a career at a library, I've cultivated a deep appreciation for the power of storytelling, whether it be through the pages of a book or the magic of the stage. Theatre, with its ability to transport and connect, has become a cherished passion of mine. I've created a profile on this website to immerse myself in more plays, to share my insights, and to connect with others who share my love for the arts. Let's explore the wonders of theatre together!

My Reviews

Le Miserable

January 15, 2024
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Le Miserable is an unforgettable experience that immerses you in a world of passion, sacrifice, and redemption. From the stirring musical score to the mesmerizing performances, every moment of the play resonates with raw emotion and poignant storytelling. The breathtaking set design transports you to 19th-century France, where the struggles of the characters feel both timeless and deeply personal. As the curtains close, you're left with a profound sense of awe and appreciation for the human spirit. Le Miserable is not just a play, but a transformative journey that lingers in your heart long after the final bow.

The Lion King

February 28, 2024
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A majestic spectacle that transports you to the heart of the African savannah. From the vibrant costumes to the enchanting music, every aspect of the production is a visual and auditory feast. The performances are awe-inspiring, capturing the essence of each beloved character with grace and charisma. As the timeless story unfolds on stage, you're swept away by a wave of nostalgia and wonder. The breathtaking puppetry and choreography bring the animal kingdom to life in a way that is both magical and mesmerizing. The Lion King is a true masterpiece that captivates audiences of all ages, leaving them spellbound from beginning to end.
