GEUBELS TAKES A BATH

After a few years of television work (Taboo, LOL: Last One Laughing) Geubels goes on touring the Dutch theaters again. As expected, he lets the hall singer and snoaces about recognizances from normal life. He shines his light on overpriced food trucks, Christmas decorations at funeral directors and the genesis of the croquette. From his safe bath, he also muters about loss and sadness, only to open the tap of nonsense wide.
